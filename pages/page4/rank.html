Welcome to Ranks!

In here you can determine how much MMR you will need to achieve your desired rank, Dota 2 uses medals to represent a player's skill level. These medals range from Herald, Guardian, and Crusader to Archon, Legend, Ancient, Divine, and Immortal. Each medal has five stars.

MMR is a numerical value that represents a player's skill level. It is used for matchmaking purposes. When players first start playing ranked matches, they go through calibration matches. The results of these matches determine their initial MMR and medal placement.
